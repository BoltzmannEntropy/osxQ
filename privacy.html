Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

1. Introduction

QuantumStudio ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

2. Information Collection

QuantumStudio is designed to be privacy-first. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All quantum circuit simulations and benchmarks are performed entirely on your device.

3. Local Processing

All quantum computing simulations, including QFT, VQE, and QCBM benchmarks, are executed locally on your Apple Silicon device using MLX GPU acceleration. No computation data is sent to external servers.

4. Benchmark Results

Benchmark results and performance data are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or aggregate benchmark data from users.

5. Data Security

Since all data is processed and stored locally on your device, the security of your data relies on the security of your device.

6. Third-Party Services

The application utilizes Apple's MLX framework for GPU acceleration. This is a local computation framework and does not transmit data externally.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at tripflow.contact@gmail.com.
